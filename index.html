Kyle's Blurbs
About me:
I'm Kyle Thumm. I'm the Head of Marketing at Rooftop Power Co, a solar, roofing, and HVAC company where I run a 13-person sales team across 285 zip codes in RI, MA, and CT. I manage everything from Google Ads and Meta to CRM builds in GoHighLevel, lead vendor management, and sales pipeline optimization.

On the side, I'm building handled. — an AI-powered platform for home service companies. Three AI agents: Ava (speed-to-lead & appointment setting), Stella (review & reputation management), and Scout (lead qualification & routing). It's the system I wish I had when I was running lead gen at scale.

I also run SolarGrade (a review platform for solar companies), host the Solar Growth Podcast, and do consulting through Measure3 for local businesses in Huntsville, AL.

Family man first. I've got 3 boys — Weston (7), Luke (4), and Cole (1). They're the reason I do everything. And yes, I still play hockey and hit the gym. I have a degree in Exercise Physiology from CSUN. Old habits die hard.
