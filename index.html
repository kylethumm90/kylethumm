Thinking
Ideas that shape my work.
"Most setters aren't setting appointments. They're setting churn."
On Sales Enablement

If your setters are booking unqualified appointments, your closers burn out, your no-show rate climbs, and your cost-per-sale explodes. The fix isn't more leads — it's better qualification systems and comp plans that reward quality over volume.

"Winning in solar marketing isn't about spending more. It's about wasting less."
On Solar Marketing in 2025

The companies scaling right now aren't outspending their competitors — they're out-systeming them. Better lead routing, faster follow-up, tighter attribution, and the discipline to cut channels that look busy but don't close.

"Stop checking your bank account. Start checking your to-do list."
On Founder Vulnerability

Revenue anxiety is a symptom, not the problem. The founders who win are the ones who obsess over the inputs — the daily actions, the systems, the follow-through — and let the outputs take care of themselves.
