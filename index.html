Kyle's Blurbs
About me:
I run marketing at Rooftop Power Co., a solar, roofing, and HVAC company covering RI, MA, and CT. I handle the marketing that feeds a 15+ person sales team. Google Ads, Meta, GHL, lead vendors, CRM builds, pipeline ops. All of it.

6+ years in home services. 100,000+ leads generated across 30+ states. I've seen what works and what doesn't.

Now I'm building handled., an AI-powered front office for home service contractors. Three agents: Ava (speed-to-lead and booking), Stella (reviews, referrals, reactivation), and Scout (analytics). It's the system that contractors have been asking for for years.

Family man first. I've got 3 boys: Weston (7), Luke (4), and Cole (1). They're the reason I do everything. I still play hockey twice a week and hit the gym late at night. Exercise Physiology degree from CSUN. Old habits die hard.
Who I'd Like to Meet:

AI builders who are shipping real products, not just tweeting about it. SaaS founders in the trenches. And Tom from MySpace. The OG. If you're reading this Tom, let's connect.
